VoxelVision

Medical imaging, reimagined in 3D space.

Born on Apple Vision Pro. Now on iPad and iPhone.

Most medical imaging viewers were built for desktops — even those with 3D capabilities still map volumetric navigation onto mouse clicks and scroll wheels. The data is three-dimensional, but the interaction isn't.

VoxelVision is a native spatial DICOM viewer for Apple Vision Pro, iPad, and iPhone. Your volume sits in your physical space. Navigate anatomy with natural gestures in 3D space — drag to slice, pinch to scale, reposition freely. No mouse, no translation layer between intent and action.

If you work with CT, MR, PET, or NM data — for study, research, teaching, or workflow exploration — VoxelVision brings the interaction up to the dimensionality of the data.
