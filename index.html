VoxelVision

Medical imaging, reimagined in 3D space.

Most medical imaging viewers were built for desktops — even those with 3D capabilities still map volumetric navigation onto mouse clicks and scroll wheels. The data is three-dimensional, but the interaction isn't.

VoxelVision is a native DICOM viewer for visionOS. Your volume sits in your physical space. Navigate anatomy with natural hand gestures in 3D space — drag to slice, pinch to scale, reposition freely. No mouse, no translation layer between intent and action.

If you work with CT, MR, or PET data — for study, research, teaching, or workflow exploration — VoxelVision brings the interaction up to the dimensionality of the data.
